Manage your personal finances

The Expenses App allows you to record the expenses and purchases you make by categories, and in turn consult the graph that shows which of them you invest the most money in.

Why you should use Expenses App

Easy to Use

Manage your finances by recording and classifying them into categories that you can customize to your liking. View your summary in graphs and stay informed of your transactions.

Automatable

You can record expenses or income in batches, for this you must select the start and end dates and their frequency (once a day, a week, a month, a year). You can even add future transactions that will be included when the scheduled date arrives.

Customizable

You can change the color of your categories to the one you like best. If the brightness is a problem for your eyes, access the dark mode and continue to interact with the application.

Features

  1. Register transactions

    The application allows you to record transactions of expenses or income

  2. Categorize

    You can assign categories to your transactions, in this way you will know which are the expenses or income that predominate

  3. The past, present or future transaction date

    Select the date of the transaction for any day you want. If you forgot to schedule a past expense or income, don't worry! you can schedule it now that you remember it. If you already know in advance that you are going to carry out a transaction, you can also add it, however it will be included when said date arrives.

  4. Add details

    Add detail to your transactions, such as a title to identify it or a report to describe what you want to remember about said movement.
